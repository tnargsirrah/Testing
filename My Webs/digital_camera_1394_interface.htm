Digital Camera 1394 Interface

The driver that communicates to your DV camera is (Msdv.sys) and is part of Windows SE, ME and 2000. It controls the operation of your DV camera VIA the Adaptec AFW-4300 FireConnect 1394 card.

The DV camcorder driver (Msdv.sys) is a WDM driver that accepts property sets from DirectShow interfaces and translates them into commands that a DV camcorder can process. Currently, all DV camcorders follow the function control protocol (FCP) defined by IEC 61883, Digital Interface for Consumer Electronic Audio/Video Equipment, for the transport of audio/video command requests and responses to DV camcorders attached to the IEEE 1394 bus.
